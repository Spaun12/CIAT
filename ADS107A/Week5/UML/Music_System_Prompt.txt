
Personal Music System: Requirements Model

Attributes:
- playlist: List of tracks.
- soundSettings: Equalizer and volume controls.
- connectivityStatus: Bluetooth or wired connection status.

Operations:
- playMusic(track): Plays a selected track.
- adjustVolume(level): Changes the volume.
- createPlaylist(tracks): Compiles a list of tracks into a new playlist.

Real-World Experience Considerations:
- Importance of sound quality and user-friendly interfaces for musicians.
- Features like detailed equalizer settings and easy navigation through large music libraries.
