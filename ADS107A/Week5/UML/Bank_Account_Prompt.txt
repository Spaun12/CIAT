
Bank Account: Requirements Model

Attributes:
- accountBalance: Current available balance.
- transactionHistory: Record of all transactions.
- accountType: Type of account (e.g., savings, checking).

Operations:
- deposit(amount): Adds money to the account.
- withdraw(amount): Removes money from the account.
- transferFunds(recipient, amount): Moves money to another account.

Real-World Experience Considerations:
- Security features expected in banking apps.
- Functionalities like scheduled payments, budget tracking, and fraud alerts.
